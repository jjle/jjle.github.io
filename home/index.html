Junle Jiang

Postdoctoral Scholar in Geophysics

Cornell University

Hi! I am a geophysicist, born and raised in China and trained in Southern California. I am fascinated by the dynamic processes of the Earth and strive to understand their underlying physical mechanisms and predictability.

I am broadly interested in crustal dynamics and natural hazards, with current focuses on Earth’s deformation due to faulting or human activities over time scales spanning seconds to centuries. My approach to these problems blends observational, theoretical, and computational strengths through a combination of seismological/geodetic observational techniques and laboratory-based mechanical models.
